About Chatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj

Sambhaji Maharaj hote ek veer yoddha va rajkaranik mahapurush. Ti loksansthe, maryada va dharmaacha rakshan karanyasathi parakramane ladly. Tyancha jeevan shahidyane, niti ani rajaneeti ne bharlela hota.

Achievements & Highlights

  • Shaurya: Battlefield var adhik shaurya va sahas darshavla.
  • Rajnitik dakshata: Strategy va diplomacy madhye prabhavit yogyata.
  • Sanskritik rakshan: Lok sanskruti va dharmacha rakshan kela.
  • Prerna: Aaj pan tyanchi kahani yuva pidhila prerna deti.
